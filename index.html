About Me

Hi! I'm Gummi, a Roblox Developer based in the UK (BST), with over 5 years of professional experience since 2019. Having been part of the Roblox community since 2014, I've grown alongside the platform, gaining valuable insights into player expectations and industry trends.


I'm proficient in Lua scripting for Roblox and skilled in Python for backend systems. My expertise with Roblox Studio allows me to deliver optimized and immersive gameplay experiences, handling both front-end UI design and back-end server architecture. My previous projects include creating custom combat mechanics, procedural maze generation, robust inventory systems, and engaging magic abilities.


I'm currently open to project collaborations, consulting opportunities, or full development roles. Feel free to get in touch to discuss your Roblox project!
