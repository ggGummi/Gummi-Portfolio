About Me

Hello! I'm Gummi, a professional Roblox Developer with over 5 years of specialized experience since 2019. Based in the UK (BST), I've been part of the Roblox community since 2014, giving me a comprehensive understanding of the platform's evolution and player expectations. I'm highly proficient in Lua programming for Roblox and have expertise in Python for backend development. My deep knowledge of Roblox Studio enables me to create engaging, optimized gameplay experiences from concept to completion. My portfolio includes complex game systems like custom combat mechanics, procedural maze generation, inventory systems, and immersive magic abilities. I specialize in both front-end UI development and back-end server architecture. I'm currently available for project collaborations, consulting work, or full development roles. Feel free to reach out to discuss how I can bring your Roblox vision to life!
